About me

Hi all! My name is Daine and I am 14 years old. Now I am studying in the 8th grade at the NSPMs in Almaty. I study at a music school and also do rhythmic gymnastics. I also participate in math olympiads.

My history

I started doing rhythmic gymnastics in 2014 and am still doing it. I don’t remember where I trained before 2016, but in February 2016 I moved to Natalia Kuznetsova’s school, where I still train. In addition to gymnastics, I study at a music school. I went there in 2016, when I was in 1st grade.

And so in 2019 I received 1 adult grade, and in 2021 I received a candidate master of sports in rhythmic gymnastics, while continuing to study at a music school.

But at the beginning of 2022, I learned about the NSPM school. Since I was in 6th grade, I decided to go there. Therefore, I had to leave music school and gymnastics to prepare for NSPM. I started going to the Clevers center for preparation. I couldn’t score enough points for a grant, but enough for a paid department, so I went to the 7th grade in a paid department and after 1 quarter switched to a grant.

And starting from the second 7th grade, I began to get involved in olympiad mathematics and participate in various olympiads. And also at the beginning of 8th grade, I returned to gymnastics and music school and still go there.
